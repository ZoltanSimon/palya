Hello and welcome to Generation:Football's very own Starting Eleven Builder! We felt that it is hard to find a proper football(soccer for the US based fans) Starting 11 creator tool on the internet, so we have decided to make our own. It is made entirely from scratch, no assets were used from other websites.

All you have to do is to select the formation and make your team stand out with the various styling options: You can select the shape of the players, their color or pattern, the pitch's style and many more interesting possibilities!
Fill the players' names and their numbers. The position names will be filled automatically, based on the player's position on the field. Don't hesitate to play around with various formations! More formations will be added in the future.

Don't hesitate to share your team on Twitter, Facebook, Instagram, TikTok or any other social media you can think of! You can use the Download button to download the team, thus making this process super easy.
